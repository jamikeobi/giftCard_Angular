Gift Naija Gift Cards to Employees Tax-Free

Want to treat your staff tax-free? We're here to help. HMRC's Trivial Benefits scheme lets you give every employee a tax-free gift so long as you match the below criteria:


  • it costs you £50 or less to provide
  • it isn’t cash or a cash voucher
  • it isn’t a reward for their work or performance
  • it isn’t in the terms of their contract

Not only does treating your staff tax-free in the form of a One-4-all multi-store gift card up to the value of £50 qualify as a trivial benefit, but it can also save you up to 32.5% per trivial benefit with zero national insurance or tax.
