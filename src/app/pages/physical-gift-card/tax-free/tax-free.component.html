Gift Your Staff Tax-Free with Trivial Benefits

How it works

HMRC's Trivial Benefit allowance means that businesses can gift their employees up to £50 tax-free, providing they meet the criteria below:

  • The benefit must not exceed £50 per employee (including any VAT).
  • The benefit must not be cash or a cash voucher.
  • The benefit must not be given as part of an employee's contractual or salary agreement (this includes regularity which can be assumed as a work perk).
  • The benefit must not be given as a reward for work or performance. Instead, these are perks to boost morale and surprise delight.

Choose hassle-free employee gifting by delivering a gift in the form of a One4all Multi-Store Gift Card, instead of a payment through the employee's salary.

What's more, One4all Gift Cards can be spent with over 180 retailers nationwide and online, on everything from food to fashion, homeware to holidays, giving your staff the flexibility to choose a gift they'll truly value.

Taking advantage of the Trivial Benefit tax-free allowance couldn't be easier. Simply choose a card and complete the checkout process, and your order will be dispatched, free of charge, via Special/First Class post directly to your business.
