Reward your employees tax-free with One4all Rewards

Thanks to the HMRC Trivial Benefits scheme, you can give employees a tax-free gift. So, what are trivial benefits? To qualify, employee gifts must match the following criteria:

  • It costs you £50 or less to provide
  • It isn’t cash or a cash voucher
  • It isn’t a reward for their work or performance
  • It isn’t in the terms of their contract

This means no payroll hassle, no national insurance, and no tax.
